About Our Department

The Anesthesia Department at SHP Hospital is dedicated to providing safe and effective pain management for all surgical procedures. Our team of expert anesthesiologists ensures that patients remain comfortable, stable, and pain-free during operations. We use the latest technologies and protocols to monitor vital signs and adjust anesthesia levels as needed.
