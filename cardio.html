About Our Department

The Cardiology Department at SHP Hospital specializes in diagnosing, treating, and preventing heart-related diseases. Our experienced cardiologists use state-of-the-art equipment to provide accurate diagnostics, minimally invasive procedures, and comprehensive cardiac care for patients of all ages. Your heart health is our top priority.
